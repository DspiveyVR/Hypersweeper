How to Play
Hypersweeper follows the rules of classic Minesweeper but the only difference is that the various planes represent layers of a 4D hyper-cube. Conceptually these planes are somehow layered on top of each other, which means that each square has far more adjacent squares than a typical Minesweeper game (a maximum of 80). In this game, the outlines that appear when you hover over a square serve as a guide to show you which squares are adjacent to the one you are currently hovering over. Also if you don't know how to play the original Minesweeper you should close your browser window immediately lol.....
